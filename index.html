Learn About Our Troop

Welcome to Troop 690

Troop 690 is chartered by St. William the Abbot Roman Catholic Church and is part of the East Nassau District of Scouting America Long Island Council. Through camping, hiking, service projects, leadership opportunities, and outdoor adventure, our Scouts build friendships and skills that last a lifetime.

The emphasis of our troop is youth leadership. Scouts plan meetings, organize campouts, and lead activities while adult leaders serve as mentors and advisors. This allows every Scout to grow in confidence, responsibility, and decision-making while experiencing the values of Scouting.

Scouting is also a family activity. Parents are encouraged to participate, volunteer, and support their Scout throughout their journey from Scout Rank to Eagle Scout.

Things to Know

  • Troop meetings are held every Monday at 7:30 PM.
  • We camp nearly every month throughout the year.
  • Each summer we attend resident summer camp in Upstate New York.
  • New Scouts are always welcome. Visiting a meeting is a great way to learn more before joining.

The Scout Oath and Law

Our Scouts strive to live by the Scout Oath and Scout Law every day, committing themselves to be trustworthy, loyal, helpful, friendly, courteous, kind, obedient, cheerful, thrifty, brave, clean, and reverent. These values guide our troop both in the outdoors and in everyday life.
