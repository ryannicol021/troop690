Join Troop 690

Welcome to Troop 690


Troop 690 is chartered by St. William the Abbot Roman Catholic Church in Seaford, New York. As part of the East Nassau District and Scouting America Long Island Council, our Scouts enjoy camping, hiking, canoeing, high adventure, community service, leadership opportunities, and advancement from Scout through Eagle Scout.

Why Join Troop 690?


Outdoor Adventure

Monthly campouts, hiking, canoeing, backpacking, and summer camp.

Leadership

Our troop is youth-led, helping Scouts develop confidence and responsibility.

Service

Give back through meaningful service projects throughout our community.
