Troop 690 is based in Seaford, New York and proudly serves Scouts and families from Seaford, Massapequa, Wantagh, and surrounding communities. Chartered by St. William the Abbot RC Church and part of Scouting America Long Island, our troop has been building leaders since 1962.

Welcome to Troop 690

Founded in 1962, Troop 690 has served the Seaford community for over six decades. Through outdoor adventure, leadership development, and community service, Scouts grow in character and responsibility while building lifelong friendships.

Our troop is youth-led, with Scouts planning meetings, leading patrols, and organizing campouts. Adult leaders serve as mentors, ensuring safety and guiding Scouts through the program.

Scouting is also a family experience, and parents are encouraged to stay involved in their Scout’s journey from joining through Eagle Scout.

Things to Know

• Meetings: Mondays at 7:30 PM in Seaford

• Monthly campouts throughout the year

• Summer camp in Upstate New York

• New Scouts are always welcome
